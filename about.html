About Us

We are here to provide unlimited practice question sets targeting the Indian Railway Limited departmental competitive examination for the AEN post only. In future we will cover all posts (including ACM, AEE, AFA, AME, AMM, AOM, APO, ASTE).

Questions will draw from the latest Indian Railway manuals and core‐subject material. You’ll also find a dedicated Current Affairs & GK section to keep you sharp on general topics.

This site is maintained by an AEN‐post aspirant as part of his personal preparation. Once the developer secures the AEN position, the website will be opened for public use.
